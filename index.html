About the Band

Mike and the Boyz are the most radical hair metal band to emerge from Trail, British Columbia! We bring the thunder of the 80s with face-melting guitar solos, sky-high hair, and power ballads that'll make you cry tears of pure metal.

From the mountains of BC to stages across Canada, we're keeping the spirit of 80s metal alive with our authentic sound, leather-clad style, and enough hairspray to punch a hole in the ozone. Get ready to rock!
